# MyCEO Platform - Deliverables Presentation Script

---

## Introduction

> "I developed the **MyCEO Platform**, a comprehensive Learning Management System designed for parents who want to provide their children with an engaging educational experience. The platform combines interactive learning modules, business simulation through virtual company building, and gamification to keep children motivated."

---

## Technology Stack

> "The platform was built using modern web technologies:
> 
> - **React 18** with **TypeScript** for the frontend, ensuring type safety and maintainability
> - **Vite** as the build tool for fast development
> - **TailwindCSS** for responsive, modern styling
> - **Supabase** as the backend, providing authentication, PostgreSQL database, file storage, and serverless Edge Functions
> - **Stripe** for secure payment processing and subscription management
> - The project follows a **monorepo architecture** using pnpm workspaces for better code organization"

---

## Key Deliverables

### 1. Authentication System

> "I implemented a dual authentication system:
> - **Parents** can sign up with email and password, including email verification and password reset flows
> - **Children** use unique access codes generated by parents, making it simple and secure for kids to log in
> - **Single Sign-On (SSO)** integration for cross-application authentication"

---

### 2. Subscription & Payment System

> "The platform includes a complete Stripe integration with:
> - Three subscription tiers: Basic, Standard, and Premium
> - Monthly and annual billing options
> - 30-day free trial support
> - Customer self-service portal for managing billing
> - Webhook handling for subscription lifecycle events"

---

### 3. Parent Dashboard & Child Management

> "Parents have a dedicated dashboard where they can:
> - Add and manage their children's accounts
> - Generate QR codes for easy child login
> - Track each child's learning progress
> - View achievements and performance
> - Soft delete and restore child accounts"

---

### 4. Learning Management System

> "The LMS core features include:
> - **Modular course structure** with lessons organized by topic
> - **Multiple content types**: Text, Video, Quizzes, PDFs, and Presentations
> - **Interactive quizzes** with real-time feedback
> - **Progress tracking** at both lesson and module levels
> - **Module thumbnails** and visual content delivery"

---

### 5. Gamification System

> "To keep children engaged, I implemented:
> - **XP and leveling system** that rewards activity
> - **Achievement badges** for completing milestones
> - **Daily streak tracking** to encourage consistent learning
> - **Leaderboard** for friendly competition among learners"

---

### 6. Company Builder Feature

> "A unique feature of this platform is the virtual company simulation:
> - Children can create and name their own company
> - Choose a business specialty
> - Track transactions and manage virtual finances
> - Earn achievements by reaching revenue milestones
> - This teaches entrepreneurship and financial literacy in a fun way"

---

### 7. Marketplace

> "The platform includes a marketplace where children can:
> - Browse and purchase virtual items
> - Items have quantity limits and stock tracking
> - Supports currency representation in both USD and RM"

---

### 8. Admin CMS

> "I built a complete admin panel with:
> - **Dashboard** for platform overview and statistics
> - **Module and content management** for creating and editing courses
> - **User management** to oversee all parent and child accounts
> - **Analytics dashboard** for tracking engagement
> - **Completion tracking** to monitor learner progress
> - **Feature flags** to toggle features on/off
> - **Notification system** for announcements"

---

### 9. Database & Security

> "On the backend:
> - **37 database migrations** covering all schema changes
> - **Row Level Security (RLS)** policies ensuring data isolation
> - **Database functions** for code generation, level calculation, and achievement logic
> - **Performance-optimized indexes** for fast queries
> - **Secure file storage** for profile pictures, lesson files, and marketplace images"

---

## Summary

> "In summary, the MyCEO Platform is a production-ready LMS featuring:
> - A complete authentication system for parents and children
> - Stripe-powered subscription management
> - Interactive learning modules with multiple content types
> - Gamification through XP, achievements, and leaderboards
> - A unique company-building simulation
> - A full-featured admin CMS
> - Secure database architecture with proper access controls
> 
> The platform is built with modern technologies and best practices, ready for deployment and scaling."
